Footwear / Outsole / Sand Traction

Marathon Des Sables Outsole Traction

EventMarathon Des Sables
Project TimelineBrief to final: 1 week
TerrainHot, steep sand
FocusRunning economy

This footwear concept explores outsole traction for Marathon Des Sables, a six-day, 250K stage race across the Moroccan Sahara.

The design question focused on how an outsole could keep athletes more afloat on deep and packed sand, helping reduce sinking, save energy, and improve running economy through steep sandy sections.
