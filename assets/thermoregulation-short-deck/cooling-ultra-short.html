Cooling Ultra Short

A heat-management short inspired by Western States Endurance Run, built to support cooling, storage, and efficient movement in hot terrain.

Primary UseHot ultra efforts
CoolingBuilt-in ice pocket
StorageWaistband carry

Inspired by the ice bandanas and ice pools used by athletes at Western States, these shorts integrate an ice pocket into the liner.

Ice melt targets the femoral artery and groin area to help manage core body temperature while the short itself avoids holding excess water.

Waistband storage is preserved for a soft flask, nutrition, phone, keys, and other trail essentials.

Design Intent

Keep the engine cool without sacrificing carry.

The short is built around a simple mountain-running problem: athletes need heat relief and storage at the same time. The solution cools a major blood vessel while keeping the waistband useful for fast, self-supported movement.
