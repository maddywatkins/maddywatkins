Dragonfly Midsole Cushioning

* Rendered with AI: Vizcom

BriefMidsole cushioning project inspired by insect biomimicry
Project Timeline1 week
FocusAdaptive stability

This footwear concept uses dragonfly anatomy as inspiration for a midsole cushioning system.

The research focused on independent wing movement, tail stabilization, and lightweight lattice structures, translating those ideas into a footwear concept centered on adaptive support and efficient movement.
