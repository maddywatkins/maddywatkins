Trail Shirt / Sun Protection / Lightweight Layer

Cooling Sun Button-Up

A lightweight button-up trail shirt designed to keep sun off the arms during big days up high without feeling heavy or restrictive.

Primary UseHigh-output trail days
NeedSun coverage
FocusLightweight comfort

The shirt is designed for long days at altitude, where athletes need breathable coverage more than a heavy protective layer.

The button-up format supports venting and adjustability while keeping the silhouette familiar, casual, and easy to wear on and off trail.

The project balances sun protection, movement, and a playful trail-ready visual language.

Design Intent

A sun layer that still wants to move.

The concept treats sun protection as part of a fast-moving trail system: light coverage, easy ventilation, and enough comfort to stay on through exposed ridgelines, long climbs, and changing mountain conditions.
